# Product Requirements Document - conscious-founder

**Author:** Rabbit
**Date:** 2026-01-08

## Executive Summary

The Conscious-Founder Module bridges intuition-driven discovery with structured content creation by wrapping the existing K2M Attention Control Mode and 4-agent architecture into a BMAD-native module with structured injection points and return protocols. Unlike typical AI writing tools that turn humans into reviewers, this system preserves the "boiling phase" where intuition compounds‚Äîadding structure at the right moments (before workflow execution and after publishing) while respecting non-linear, memory-based creative processes.

The module converts standalone agent documents into invoke-able BMAD agents, adds a pre-workflow "inject" workflow to capture emphasis/angle before K2M runs, a post-publish "return" workflow for re-entering knowledge nodes and deepening insights, and a "repurpose" workflow to extract social posts from published newsletters.

### What Makes This Special

The critical design principle: **"The tool accelerates execution but must never replace the boiling phase where intuition does what AI can't: compound connections across time in your own cognitive architecture."** The module adds structure AROUND the creative process, not attempts to replace the intuitive compounding phase. Unlike systems that make humans reviewers of AI-generated content, this maintains human judgment at critical checkpoints while preserving the non-linear "node-orbiting" pattern where insights compound across NotebookLM sessions over time.
